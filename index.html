Najun Niu (牛娜君)

I am a Ph.D. student at East China Normal University (ECNU). This homepage records the events that occurred during my graduate studies.
My main research interests include image restoration (image super-resolution, image dehazing, image denoising, image demosaicing, and image enhancement) based on deep learning.
My google scholar is: Juncheng Li (李俊诚)
